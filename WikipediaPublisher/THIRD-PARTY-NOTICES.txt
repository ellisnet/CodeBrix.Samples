THIRD-PARTY NOTICES
===================

This file documents third-party content that is bundled with, or used at run
time by, the WikipediaPublisher sample application, together with the licenses
under which that content is provided.

Third-party CODE dependencies are consumed as NuGet packages. Each package
carries its own license and third-party notices in its own repository/package
(the CodeBrix.* packages ship their own THIRD-PARTY-NOTICES.txt), so those are
not reproduced here.

Wikipedia article content and images (downloaded at run time)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
This sample fetches article text and images from Wikipedia
(https://www.wikipedia.org) at run time and composes them into a PDF.

Wikipedia article text is licensed under the Creative Commons
Attribution-ShareAlike license (CC BY-SA;
https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/). Article images carry their
own individual licenses, as noted on their Wikimedia description pages. Each
generated PDF includes a colophon carrying the article's attribution.

Excepting a few sample output documents, none of this content is redistributed
as part of this repository; it is fetched on demand.
